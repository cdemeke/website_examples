# Corporate Style

## Overview

Corporate websites communicate professionalism, trust, and stability. They use structured layouts, professional imagery, and clear messaging to build confidence with potential clients and stakeholders.

## Key Characteristics

- **Professional color schemes** (blues, grays, greens)
- **Structured grid layouts** with clear sections
- **Trust indicators** (testimonials, logos, certifications)
- **Professional photography** of teams and offices
- **Clear CTAs** for demos, contact, or consultations
- **Comprehensive navigation** with dropdowns

## Prompts

### Enterprise Corporate Site
```
Create a professional corporate website with a navy blue
and white color scheme. Include a mega-menu navigation,
hero section with a business value proposition, client
logo bar, services grid, testimonial carousel, and a
footer with comprehensive links. Use professional stock
imagery of diverse business teams.
```

### B2B Services Site
```
Design a B2B corporate website focused on building trust.
Include case study cards, an "About Us" section with team
photos, industry certifications, and a prominent contact
form. Use a clean blue and gray palette with green accent
for CTAs.
```

### Financial Services Site
```
Build a corporate website for a financial services company.
Emphasize security and trust with badges, client testimonials,
and clear service explanations. Use a conservative color
palette with navy, gold accents, and plenty of whitespace.
Include data visualizations and charts.
```
